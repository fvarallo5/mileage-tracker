TrekTrack Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

TrekTrack helps gig workers and companies log audit-ready trip mileage, tips, and expense reports. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Location data: When you start GPS tracking, we use your device location to calculate trip distance. Background location may be used during active trips or while auto-detect monitoring is enabled. You can turn this off anytime in app Settings.
  • Trip data: Dates, miles driven, tips/earnings, notes, and import source (e.g. Uber, DoorDash) that you enter or import.
  • Settings: Your preferred IRS mileage reimbursement rate, battery mode, and theme preference.

How We Use Your Data

  • To calculate mileage, expenses, and earnings reports
  • To store and sync your trips via Supabase (secure cloud database)
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties

Data Storage

Trip data is stored in Supabase, a hosted PostgreSQL database with row-level security. You can delete individual trips within the app at any time.

Third-Party Services

If you import CSV files from Uber, DoorDash, Lyft, or Instacart, that data is parsed and stored in your account. We are not affiliated with these companies. Cloud infrastructure is provided by Supabase.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at support@trektrack.app.

Children

This app is not intended for users under 18.
